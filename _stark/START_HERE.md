## 👀 See the best parts (about 60 seconds)

1. **Open the app** (double-click `Open StrikeSim 2040.command`), then click **Map** (top-right).
2. **Look at the picture.** Real military symbols (red diamonds = enemy, blue = friendly), on a
   clean dark map, with **threat rings** showing missile/air-defense/sensor reach. Top-right of
   the map you can switch **Dark / Satellite** and toggle the **Engagement zones**.
3. **Play a turn.** Click **⚔ War Game** (top center) → Blue = Human, Red = Computer → Start.
   Take down the enemy's **Command** and **Logistics** units and watch their action points drop —
   that's the new "cut their tempo" mechanic.
